===== PAGE 9 =====
Information in a nutshell
The original sign error from 1985 leads to:
Large errors for low aerosol loads and/or long integration pathways.
Smaller but substantial errors, especially in boundary layer conditions
(plenty of aerosol, short integration pathways).
Complex error behavior, preventing a-posteriori corrections.
Fernald 1984 uses a slightly different notation, but with correct signs.
